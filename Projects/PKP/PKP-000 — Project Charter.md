# PKP-000 — Project Charter

## Purpose

The Personal Knowledge Platform (PKP) is a long-term engineering initiative to create a permanent, trusted, and vendor-independent repository for my knowledge.

The PKP serves as the single source of truth for curated knowledge accumulated through learning, professional experience, research, experimentation, and personal projects.

Its purpose is not merely to store information, but to transform information into reusable knowledge that supports better thinking, decision-making, continuous learning, and practical implementation.

---

# Vision

Build a world-class Personal Knowledge Platform that remains valuable for decades regardless of changes in software, AI platforms, or technology trends.

Knowledge belongs to me.

AI assists with reasoning.

The PKP remains the permanent source of truth.

---

# Mission

Design, build, and continuously improve a Git-first, Markdown-first, AI-first knowledge platform that enables:

* Long-term knowledge retention
* Practical application of knowledge
* Continuous learning
* Cross-domain knowledge reuse
* AI-assisted reasoning
* Complete ownership of personal knowledge

---

# Success Criteria

The PKP is considered successful if it:

* Becomes the primary repository for curated knowledge.
* Supports efficient retrieval by both humans and AI assistants.
* Prevents unnecessary duplication of knowledge.
* Encourages practical implementation rather than passive collection.
* Evolves without vendor lock-in.
* Remains understandable and maintainable over decades.

---

# Core Principles

The PKP follows these principles:

* Knowledge First
* Markdown First
* Git First
* AI First
* Vendor Independence
* Offline First
* Documentation Driven Development
* Single Source of Truth
* Progressive Domain Evolution
* Human Readable
* AI Readable
* Long-term Maintainability

---

# Scope

The PKP includes:

* Knowledge
* Resources
* Projects
* Architecture
* Standards
* Templates
* Automation
* AI integration
* Research
* Decision records

---

# Out of Scope

The PKP is not intended to become:

* A task management system
* A calendar
* A document management system
* A cloud-dependent platform
* A proprietary AI product

Specialized tools may be integrated where appropriate, but the PKP remains the authoritative knowledge repository.

---

# Governance

Knowledge is created and curated through the following workflow:

Resource

↓

Capture

↓

Process

↓

Knowledge

↓

Implementation

↓

Reflection

↓

Continuous Improvement

AI may assist throughout this process, but all permanent knowledge is subject to human review before becoming part of the PKP.

---

# Long-Term Direction

The PKP will evolve incrementally through practical use.

Architecture, standards, templates, and automation will be refined based on real-world experience rather than theoretical design.

The platform is expected to support multiple AI services, local AI runtimes, future AI agents, and future knowledge management technologies while preserving compatibility with existing knowledge.
