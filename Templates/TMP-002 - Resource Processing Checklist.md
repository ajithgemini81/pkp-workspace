# Resource Processing Checklist

## While Consuming the Resource

- [ ] Capture important ideas without worrying about organization.
- [ ] Highlight only ideas that seem valuable.
- [ ] Write questions that come to mind.
- [ ] Note practical examples.

---

## After Completing the Resource

- [ ] Review all captured notes.
- [ ] Remove information that is not useful.
- [ ] Group similar ideas together.
- [ ] Identify reusable concepts.

---

## Knowledge Creation

For each important concept:

- [ ] Does a Knowledge Article already exist?
    - Yes → Update it.
    - No → Create it.

---

## Implementation

For each major idea ask:

- What will I change in my life?
- What will I change at work?
- What experiment will I try?

---

## Reflection

After applying the ideas:

- What worked?
- What didn't?
- What changed my understanding?

Update the Knowledge Article if necessary.
